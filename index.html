Brian Nguyen

I'm from the Bay — San Jose to be specific.

Before this, I was working on start ups for Advance Publications, and before that I was taking pictures and making visuals for the Chicago Tribune.

I'm pretty much self taught in journalism — it's my first time taking journalism classes of any kind. Originally when I first graduated, I was planning on grad school, but it just never happened. I kept looking for the chance to go but one thing led to the next (start ups will do that) and then it felt like grad school was never gonna happen.

Along the way, I started becoming obsessed with how open source investigations were being used in stories — first with Bellingcat, then with the New York Times visual foresics team. I couldn't get it out of my head. I applied to see where it would take me, and now here I am.
